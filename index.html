About

I work at the intersection of machine learning engineering and technology consulting, currently as a Senior Software Engineer in a consulting practice. Most of my day is applied: turning messy, real-world problems into models and pipelines that hold up in production.

My work spans NLP and language models (classification, natural language inference, RAG pipelines, MCP-based tooling), document AI for legal and compliance use cases, demand forecasting, and applied cybersecurity research and reporting. Alongside consulting, I take on freelance Python and ML work for legal-tech and data-heavy clients — most of it ends up on my GitHub.

I care about the parts people skip: whether a model actually beats a simple baseline, where it quietly fails, and how to explain those limits plainly.
